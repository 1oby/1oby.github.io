About Me

Hi, I'm an 18-year-old photographer based in the beautiful county of Kent, UK. My passion for photography started at a young age, and it has grown into something I truly love and dedicate my time to.

I specialize in landscape photography, capturing the stunning natural beauty that surrounds us. From rolling hills to dramatic coastlines, I love exploring Kent and beyond to find the perfect shot. I'm also a licensed drone pilot, which allows me to capture unique aerial perspectives that showcase landscapes from breathtaking heights.

Cars have been another passion of mine since childhood, and this naturally evolved into automotive photography. I enjoy capturing the sleek lines, powerful presence, and intricate details that make each vehicle unique. Whether it's a classic car at a local meet or a modern supercar on the streets, I love the challenge of photographing these mechanical works of art.
