About Me

Hi, I'm an 18-year-old photographer based in Kent, UK. I specialize in landscape photography, capturing the stunning natural beauty that surrounds us, and I'm also a licensed drone pilot which allows me to capture unique aerial perspectives.

Cars have been a passion of mine since childhood, naturally evolving into automotive photography. I love capturing the sleek lines and powerful presence that make each vehicle unique.
